# Restoring System

### Required Reading

This is a section for users who bought a second-hand PS4 or are unsure whether their console has had a jailbreak ran on it before.

### Instructions

#### Section I - Initializing your PS4

1. Open the Settings menu
1. Navigate to `Initialization` -> `Initialize PS4`
1. Press <Btn btn="confirm" /> on "quick initialization"
1. When prompted, select "Initialize", then select "Yes" to confirm 3 times
1. Once the format has finished, follow the on-screen instructions
1. Do the initial setup
